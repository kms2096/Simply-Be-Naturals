About Us

After many years of “allergic reactions” and skin irritation, I had ruled out almost every product on the market. From face wash to lotions, nothing was safe! So, I started learning to make my own products. I knew there had to be a way to make products without any of the harsh chemicals and fragrances, and there was! Once I started making my own products, I thought more people could benefit from this chemical-free body product solution.

That’s why I started Simply Be Naturals! I believe everyone should know what is in the products they use on their body and in their home! At Simply Be Naturals we make all-natural products, with ingredients you can trust so you can…Simply Be!

Here at Simply Be Naturals, every ingredient has a purpose.
